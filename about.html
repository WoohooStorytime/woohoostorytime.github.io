Mrs. Ha is the real life Ms. H from the Roys Bedoys stories. She is the voice and author of the videos, and she is also a kindergarten teacher who's passionate about designing varieties of curriculum to help children learn in a fun way. Our goal for Woohoo Storytime is to build foundational skills for ages 3 and up by using interactive games for sight words and phonics that will promote engagement. We hope this website will make each child's learning richer and more enjoyable.
